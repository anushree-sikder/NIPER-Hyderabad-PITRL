Principal Investigator

Dr. Saurabh Srivastava

He specializes in cancer nanobiology, nanomedicine, NDDS, and topical & dermal formulations. He has extensive experience in translational research, product development, technology transfer, and project management, with strong expertise in regulatory affairs and intellectual property. His work bridges innovative science and industry needs, guiding pharmaceutical products from concept to large-scale production while mentoring future scientists and fostering interdisciplinary collaboration.
