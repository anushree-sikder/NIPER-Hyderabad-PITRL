Lab Facilities

Core Laboratories

Formulation Development Lab

State-of-the-art lab for creating and optimizing a wide range of pharmaceutical dosage forms, from nano-carriers to complex parenteral systems.

Cell Culture Suite

A BSL-2 certified facility for mammalian cell culture, enabling in-vitro disease modeling, cytotoxicity assays, and cellular uptake studies.

Advanced Instrumentation

High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC)

Equipped with multiple detectors (UV, PDA, FLD) for quantitative analysis, stability studies, and impurity profiling of drug substances and products.

Dynamic Light Scattering (DLS)

For precise measurement of particle size, zeta potential, and molecular weight, crucial for characterizing nanomaterials and biomolecules.

Pre-Clinical Facilities

Small Animal Imaging Suite

Non-invasive imaging technologies to visualize and track disease progression and therapeutic response in real-time within living organisms.

Collaborative Spaces

Innovation Hub & Meeting Rooms

Dedicated spaces designed to foster brainstorming, interdisciplinary collaboration, and strategic planning with academic and industry partners.
