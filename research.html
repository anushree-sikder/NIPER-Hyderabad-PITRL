Research at PITRL

PITRL conducts interdisciplinary, translational research focused on innovative pharmaceutical technologies, nanomedicine, and disease-targeted drug delivery systems, bridging laboratory innovation with real-world impact.

Neuro Research

Advanced nanotechnology-driven strategies for neurological disorders, focusing on brain-targeted delivery systems and translational neurotherapeutics.
